Harimohan Government High School

The Harimohan Government High School is one the distinguished and earliest educational organization at the Kathal Bagicha area of the Chapainawabganj District in Bangladesh. The School was founded by prominent educationist in 1895. The school is established in enormous green land areas. The school has completed registered with Rajshahi education board and its registration no is EIIN: 124506.

The Harimohan Government High School is a famous secondary educational academy at Chapainawabganj in Bangladesh. The Institute got the acknowledgment for keeping a sound and enhanced atmosphere for the flourishing of the students and for stringent discipline. It is a finest ranked educational institute in Bangladesh. It has been playing an exceptional role in advancing the educational and cultural sector of the district. Almost 1350 students are learning in the institute under supervision of 45 skilled teachers and staffs.

Facilities in the Harimohan Government High School in Chapainawabganj

  1. Large campus covered by the amazing natural elements.
  2. Green and large schoolyard for students.
  3. Wonderful infrastructure of the school.
  4. Sufficient skillful and high educated teacher and trainer.
  5. Large and modern Science Lab in the school.
  6. Digital Lab for technology study and online class.
  7. Cultural club for extra curriculum.
  8. Scout team, BNCC team and trainer in the schools.
  9. Innovation club in the school.
  10. Sports and culture center in academy.

Admission Requirement of the Harimohan Government High School

  1. Birth Certificate of the student
  2. Passport size photograph
  3. Birth Certificate of the parents.
  4. No Objection Certificate from previous institute or last educational certificate
